# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

kexpense ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

kexpense is a fully offline application. All data you enter, including:
- Financial transactions (income and expenses)
- Transaction descriptions and categories
- Dates and amounts
- Any other information you provide

is stored **exclusively on your device** using local SQLite database storage. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (e.g., via PDF export).

### No Data Collection

We do **not** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal information
- Financial data
- Device information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Any other user data

## How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use, share, or sell any information about you or your usage of the App.

## Data Storage and Security

- All your data is stored locally on your device
- Data is stored in a SQLite database on your device
- We do not have access to your data
- Your data remains private and secure on your device
- If you uninstall the App, all local data will be deleted

## Third-Party Services

kexpense does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or transmit your data. The App operates completely offline.

## Data Export

The App provides functionality to export your data to PDF format. This export is performed entirely on your device, and you have full control over:
- What data to export
- When to export
- Where to share the exported files

We do not have access to exported files.

## Children's Privacy

kexpense does not knowingly collect information from children. The App is intended for users of all ages, but we do not collect any data from any users, including children.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. All your data is stored locally on your device, and you have complete control over it.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

## Consent

By using kexpense, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since the App operates entirely offline and does not collect any data, your use of the App does not involve any data processing by us.
